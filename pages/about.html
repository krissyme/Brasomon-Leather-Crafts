How leather is created.

Leather's unique soft material is hard to replicate. It is created from the hide of an animal. They are cleaned to remove the hair and some other debris. They are stretched and tanned through a chemical procedure. Depending on the procedure and chemical used, the kind of leather produced varies from the production to another - it can be from soft leather to suede, to hard rigid leather depending on how it's treated.
